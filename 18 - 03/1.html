    1. Crunchy Kanda Karam Podi Recipe Kanda Karam Podi is a traditional homemade spice powder that is an absolute favourite in our family. This crispy, spicy podi blends well with hot idlis, dosas, or even ghee rice, making it a versatile side dish. The best part is its delightful crunch, which enhances any dish it accompanies. Additionally, it can be sprinkled over potato fries, banana fries, or any other fried recipes for an extra burst of flavour.


      2. Ingredients
    2. 350 gms Yam
    3. 3-4 tbsp Oil (for frying yam)
    4. 2 tbsp Oil (for roasting other ingredients)
    5. 3 tbsp Peanuts
    6. 1 tbsp Chana Dal
    7. 8-10 Dry Red Chillies
    8. 12-15 Garlic Pods
    9. 1/2 tbsp Coriander Seeds
    10. 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
    11. 1 tbsp Sesame Seeds
    12. Salt (to taste)
    13. 1/2 tbsp Jaggery (optional)
    14. Small Gooseberry-sized Tamarind
